Cancellation & Refund Policy

Information on donation terms, cancellations, and support rules.

1. Introduction

1.1 Policy Scope: This Cancellation and Refund Policy governs all donations, recurring subscriptions, and contributions made through the official website of NayePankh Foundation, a registered non-profit organization under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, with valid 12A and 80G certifications.

1.2 Strict No-Refund: By making a donation or subscribing to a recurring donation plan, you acknowledge and agree to this strict no-refund policy.

1.3 NGO Project Operations: NayePankh Foundation works in areas such as education, healthcare, hygiene, women empowerment, and relief for underprivileged communities. All donations received are immediately allocated and utilized toward ongoing projects.

2. Statement of No Refund for Donations

2.1 Final Transactions: All donations made to NayePankh Foundation are final, voluntary, and non-refundable.

2.2 Gateway Deployment: Once a donation is processed through Razorpay or any payment gateway linked to our website, the amount is instantly deployed for charitable purposes.

2.3 Policy Applicability: Neither partial nor full refunds will be provided, regardless of the reason for the request.

3. Why Refunds Aren't Possible

Charitable funds are used immediately upon receipt to support urgent causes such as:

  • Providing daily meals to children and families.
  • Funding school fees and supplies for underprivileged students.
  • Hygiene campaigns and sanitary pad distribution.
  • Emergency relief work during crises and natural disasters.

Since the money is instantly allocated, reversing or refunding contributions would jeopardize ongoing projects and harm beneficiaries who rely on timely support. Donors are requested to carefully verify their details and willingness before completing transactions.

4. Subscription Cancellations (Step-by-Step)

NayePankh Foundation offers recurring donation subscriptions via Razorpay. These are ongoing contributions automatically charged at fixed intervals (monthly/quarterly/yearly).

4.1 How to Cancel: Donors may cancel future charges at any time by:

  1. Logging into their Razorpay portal and canceling the subscription directly.
  2. Emailing us at contact@nayepankh.com with their registered email address and Subscription ID.

4.2 Timeline: Cancellations may take 3–5 working days to be fully updated and reflected in the Razorpay system.

4.3 Exemption Examples:

  • Example A: If your monthly subscription is ₹500 and you cancel on the 10th of the month, the payment already processed for that month is non-refundable, but no further charges will occur in the next cycle.
  • Example B: If you cancel after an annual donation of ₹5,000 has been processed, that contribution remains non-refundable, but future annual renewals will be halted.

5. Chargebacks & Disputes

If a donor raises a chargeback or dispute with their card issuer or bank, NayePankh Foundation will provide proof of donation authorization and direct fund utilization. Since donations are voluntary, non-commercial, and instantly applied, chargebacks will generally be denied by banking channels after review. Donors are advised to contact us first regarding transaction queries.
