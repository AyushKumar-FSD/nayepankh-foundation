Overview

NayePankh Foundation (“Foundation,” “we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of every donor, supporter, volunteer, and visitor who engages with our website www.nayepankh.com. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with our services, make donations, or subscribe to our programs.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, postal address, and PAN card number (for issuing 80G tax receipts).

1.2 Payment Information: Payment transactions are handled securely via Razorpay. We collect transaction metadata (amount, date, mode of payment). We do not store your credit card details, CVV, or net banking passwords on our servers.

1.3 Usage Information: We may collect non-personal analytics data such as IP address, browser type, and duration of site visits to improve website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and acknowledge your donations/subscriptions.
  • To issue 80G tax exemption receipts and comply with legal requirements.
  • To send updates, impact reports, newsletters, and thank-you messages.
  • To respond to your queries, feedback, and support requests.
  • To comply with obligations under Indian law and regulations.

3. Consent & Data Retention

When you provide us with personal information to complete a donation or subscription, you consent to our collection and use of that information for the stated purpose. You may withdraw your consent at any time by writing to us at contact@nayepankh.com, subject to legal, contractual, and tax compliance obligations under Indian laws.

Donation and subscriber records are retained for as long as required to comply with Indian tax and accounting audits.

4. Security Measures

We adopt appropriate technical and organizational security measures to safeguard your personal data, including:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption on all website page loads and payment requests.
  • Limited administrative access to donor records on a strict need-to-know basis.
  • Intrusion detection systems and firewalls to secure hosting nodes.
