1. Introduction

1.1 General Rule: This Shipping and Exchange Policy governs all acknowledgments, certificates, digital reports, and other materials provided by NayePankh Foundation to donors, supporters, and subscribers.

1.2 Digital Focus: Since NayePankh Foundation operates primarily as a charitable organization, physical products are rarely involved, and most donor recognition materials are delivered digitally.

1.3 Donations Excluded: Donations and subscriptions do not qualify as goods, so concepts of shipping, return, or exchange do not apply to the funds.

2. Applicability

This policy applies to:

  • Digital certificates (Appreciation Certificates, Internship Completion, Letters of Recommendation, and 80G tax receipts).
  • Digital thank-you letters or acknowledgment tokens.
  • Physical items or merchandise provided in rare donor milestones.

3. Acknowledgment Materials

Donors may receive acknowledgment in the form of e-certificates, thank-you messages, and periodic impact reports showing the use of funds. All digital acknowledgments are delivered electronically via email or downloadable link. Donors must provide a valid email address; incorrect details may result in delivery delays.

4. Shipping Policy for Physical Materials

In rare campaigns, NayePankh Foundation may ship physical appreciation items or campaign merchandise.

  • Domestic Shipments (India): Delivered within 7–14 working days.
  • International Shipments: Delivered within 14–30 working days depending on customs and local courier partners.

The Foundation is not responsible for logistics delays due to incorrect shipping addresses, customs clearance, or force majeure events (natural disasters, political disturbances, strikes).

5. Exchange Policy for Merchandise

5.1 Non-Exchangeable Funds: Since donations are contributions and not commercial purchases, exchanges do not apply to funds.

5.2 Damaged Physical Items: For physical campaign merchandise received, exchanges are possible only if the item is damaged or defective in transit. The donor must notify us within 7 days of receipt at contact@nayepankh.com with photographic proof of the defect. Replaced items will be dispatched after verification.

5.3 Lost Shipments: Items lost in transit due to incorrect address submissions by the donor are not eligible for replacement.
